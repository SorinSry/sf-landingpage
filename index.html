About us

What Services we provide

We are equipped to help you brain storm ideas and create a unique log cabin design that is perfect for you.

01

Quality Construction

We believe that quality, efficient cabins are constructed with protection, precision, and purpose. With our large warehouse, we are able to construct your modular cabin any time of the year, regardless of the weather.

02

Reliable Delivery & Setup

Delivery day is the most exciting day in the entire journey. Once the “in house” construction is nearly finished, we begin to prepare everything for delivery day. You will literally be able to watch your cabin come to life right in front of your own eyes. Our goal is to safely deliver and assemble your cabin as efficiently as we can.

03

Lasting Memories

Whether it becomes your full time residence or simply a vacation home, you won’t regret spending your time in these luxurious log cabins – especially once you hear your friends rave about it! Our goal is to create an enjoyable, relaxing space where you will be able to spend every minute of every day like you never have before!
